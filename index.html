About me

My name is Nicholas Tranquilli, I am currently a junior at Central Connecticut State University and pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I have been programming since I was 15 years old and started with C++. Over the years I grew to love the language as well as programming as a whole and decided to pursue this as a professional career. I enjoy learning and I like to share what I learn with others in hopes that they will learn from my successes and failures or simply be inspired to start a new project. This is one of the major reasons why I made this site. The other reason is to just simply showcase a list of my projects and ideas all in one place.
